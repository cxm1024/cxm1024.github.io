  • f(x)=\begin{cases} 1&\text{if }x=1\text{ or }x=2 \\ f(x-1)+f(x-2)&\text{if } x>2 \end{cases} : \[\color{blue}f(x)=\begin{cases} 1&\text{if }x=1\text{ or }x=2 \\ f(x-1)+f(x-2)&\text{if } x>2 \end{cases}\]
